This is
"Desa Panglipuran"

Penglipuran Village is one of the traditional villages located in Bangli Regency, Bali Province, Indonesia.
Penglipuran village is famous as one of the tourist destinations in Bali because of its society who still preserve their traditional culture in their daily lives.

The Beauty of Penglipuran
Tourism Village

Penglipuran Village is located in Bangli Regency, Bali Province with a very strategic location. Currently it is known as the Traditional Village, where in accordance with the designation, tourists who visit this unique village will find some very interesting objects.

Some very special buildings found in Penglipuran Village, buildings with this ancient or classic category are Angkul-angkul or in Indonesian called the gate, which is made similarly between one gate and another gate.

History of Panglipuran
Traditional Village

Each region or village certainly has a historical trajectory, which is usually written in a papyrus, chronicles, inscriptions and others. Likewise, the Penglipuran Traditional Village has its own historical trajectory. Penglipuran Traditional Village, which is one of the neighborhoods of Kubu Village, the majority of the population comes from Bayung Gede Village, Kintamani District, Dati II Regency Bangli.

In the era of the Bangli kingdom, the residents of Bayung Gede Village were often assigned by the king of Bangli to participate in war and other activities in the kingdom.
